Sincere Project

Sichuan is not as frosty as the Northern, and not as tender as the Southern. But the wind just stopped here, the tree shadow whirled in the charm of the Southwest. Not to mention local delicacies - there are delicious food anywhere: hot pot, rice cakes, sweet water noodles, Long ravioli, Dandan noodles... But it cannot be ignored that exceptional number of outflow migrant workers in rural Sichuan have resulted in the largest group of left-behind children in China.

To improve the rural education in Sichuan, the Sichuan team of the Beyond the Pivot [BTP]- Feinimoshu [It’s You] was formally established in 2010. We carry out targeted innovation education, health knowledge promotion, quality development, inspirational speech and other activities, through long-term contact to bring children more help. In the past decade, Feinimoshu has maintained close contact with many schools in Sichuan Province, and has sent volunteer teams to rural schools in Xuanhan, Fanzhuo, Xinnong and Linshui for many times to carry out teaching activities. On the occasion of ten years, we have received strong support from the Sichuan Provincial Department of Education and will continue to support education in Wenchuan and other places.

Based on the principle of encouraging originality and innovation, we carry out a wide variety of curriculum activities in local primary and middle schools in Sichuan Province. Volunteers exploit and extend the popularization of extra-linguistic cultural knowledge in accordance with the curriculum syllabus in order to let local students take further steps in the main subjects.We also bring along numerous specialized courses with distinct themes and topics, such as physiological hygiene, career planning, scientific knowledge, music and fine arts, habit cultivation, life health, etc. These courses aim to expand the horizons of local students and concentrates on the cultivation of students' comprehensive quality. In order to have a better understanding of the basic situation of local students and adjust the curriculum design in a timely manner, we created the unique "1+1" mode of the Sichuan team, that is, the primary school + junior high school mode, which also gives the volunteers more challenges and fun.

“Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire” No matter how glamorous the form is, it is always the blessing from one heart to another. To improve both the physical and mental well-being of local students, we have applied an interactive sexuality course with Tree-Hole Program where they learn how to protect their body and express their concerns.

To promote kids’ comprehensive development, we have organized extracurricular activities like speech contest and sports day where they explore their potential in various fields. To provide them with consistent care, we have combined long-term contact with short-term teaching where they receive continuous love and warmth.
