Yonder Narnia Project

What does it mean to be a volunteer teacher? We have been seeking the answer to this question.

Nothing to do with fame nor gain, we wish to compose the purest poetry of being with children. To lend an ear to them; to give a hand to them, cherishing a genuine and honest purpose. We wish to progress with like-minded colleagues and turn towards Yunnan, the South of Colorful Clouds together, to bring changes to the beautiful mountains, to devote a heart for a heart and an effort for an effort. Abovementioned is what we have for the answer.

The Yonder Narnia Project is a voluntary teaching project for college students which concentrates on and dedicates to the improvement of rural education conditions in Yunnan province, China. It was founded by Beyond the Pivot in 2010 and has been working closely with Teaching Corps in North-west Yunnan, a non-government organization specializing in aid education. The main beneficiaries of Yonder Narnia Project are primary schools in Ninglang, Lijiang, China. Till now, the Project groups have conducted 17 on-the-spot teaching activities.

The altitude of Ninglang Country is very high, being 3000 meters, which makes people feel awestruck. The climate of Ninglang is dry, being a test of living for people. The journey to Ninglang Country is extraordinarily far, yet the connection among hearts cannot be blocked. Come to Ninglang, for the most genuine and pure human nature.

Over the past ten years, leaders of the Yonder Narnia Project have reached Ninglang again and again. We have penetrated children's daily life and spiritual world and tried our best to enhance their happiness: we have carried out various specialized courses such as origami courses, scientific experiments, career planning and courses on other topics. We have also cultivated children's hobbies and interests: doing readings before bed, playing chess. With great enthusiasm, we have participated in local special activities for one and another: performance and Children's day, the celebration for Yi people's New Year.

At the same time, however, we also understand that short-term interest cultivation may not bring far-reaching impact. What needed by the children are habits and companionship. Therefore, we continuously explore and try: we innovatively carry out distance teaching (Wanmachang Primary school distant teaching plan), which aims to cultivate the habit of English learning. To help solve the predicament that sex education is lacked in the rural area, we carried out health classes for different genders, so that the boys and girls can realize their physical changes timely. We work together with fund enterprises cooperated to launch public welfare projects, and we collaborated with Yi Angel Fund and Oral Health Foundation to launch smile angel oral hygiene education activities.

What's more, we distributed toothpaste and toothbrush during voluntary teaching to cultivate children's oral hygiene habits. We cooperated with Yunnan Baiyao to distribute sanitary pads and other health necessities to the girls to help the girls thrive. We also insist on communicating with children regularly and try our best to extend the influence of short-term voluntary teaching

Alongside our career of supporting education, we also hope to gain a more in-depth insight into the status quo of countryside education. Therefore, time after time, we climb up to the mountains and integrate into the countryside, visiting nearby villages and towns in our spare time besides teaching. Through this process, we collect first-hand information about the local community, perceive a more precise comprehension of the countryside phenomenon, the 'Taking targeted measures in poverty alleviation' policy, as well as the social problem of the urban-rural income gap and other social and economic issues.

Yunnan, a gorgeous wonderland, is not a place of material gain and self-benefit, but a place of love and kindness. Here, you will meet the most innocent children, harvest the most wholehearted sentiment, and have a touch of the most realistic China. Moreover, an incredible story is always waiting ahead, adventuring through uncertainties and exploring the unknown, sharing the weal and woe with the friends you meet.

On the journey to the Southwest of Colorful Clouds, we are always marching on the way. Are you interested in joining our mission, towards the direction of dream and love?
