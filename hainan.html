Hello Neverland Project

Hello Neverland Project is a volunteer teaching project launched by BTP since 2009, as well as one of the earliest three projects of BTP. With seasonal changes hurrying on, groups of project leaders and volunteers came to Hainan for beautiful encounters with children, passing on love from generation to generation. Year in and year out, we have yielded the joy and experienced failure. What left behind is the fruitful harvest and full expectations for the future.

Hello Neverland Project always adheres to the concept of “ Holding the world within your heart with visions and steps.” Setting morality, intelligence, physique, and aesthetics as goals, we persist in the people-oriented and have rooted in rural areas of Hainan Province over these years. We aspire to render knowledge and hope to students in mountainous regions, contribute to the solutions of practical difficulties within local schools, and bring unique experiences and comprehension to volunteers.

After experiencing the hardships and setbacks of volunteer teaching, Hello Neverland Project team has formed its unique teaching modes and methods based on the actual condition, for better adaption to the teaching situation in mountainous areas of Hainan. In recent years, with the rapid educational development in Hainan Province, we have been adjusting educational direction with a long-term vision, endowing new connotations to short-term volunteer education projects.

Although our teaching methods change as circumstances change, our original intention remains the same. Defining short-term teaching as the core, we render instruction to mountainous area students through face-to-face teaching, helping them better understand themselves, the collective, and the state. We establish personal relationships with children as friends, providing them with proposals about the study, life, as well as the future development direction.

Based on the volunteer teaching project, Hello Neverland Project actively communicates and cooperates with schools varying in different regions and levels in Hainan. Every year, we are willing to work with new partner schools, for further enriching teaching contents, adding vitality to the team, as well as bringing care to all kinds of students. Meanwhile, we maintain good relations with former partners, who witness our growth and make joint efforts with us for education cause in Hainan.

Moreover, Hello Neverland Project has always maintained a unique project——home visit. With deeper comprehension of local families, we hope to better command knowledge and skills that children need, parents accept, and schools support to improve our teaching quality and pertinency. We also record the changes and growth of our students through the home visit, maintaining relevant data as reference for future project participants.

Although the Hello Neverland Project has gone through several years, its heart for short-term volunteer teaching is still young. Volunteers and project leaders continually inject new vitality to our team, passing our team stories from generation to generation. If you are interested in our project, please join us to create a vast sky for children with love and kindness.
